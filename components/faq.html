Frequently Asked Questions

We know that planning an event can feel overwhelming

So we’ve gathered the most common questions our clients ask. Here you’ll find clear answers about choosing designs, customizing your invitation, delivery times, and more. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to reach out — we’re always happy to help!
How do I send my digital invitation to guests?

You can easily share your invitation by sending the unique link through email, text message, or social media. Guests can open it on any device without needing an app.

Can I customize the design of my invitation?

Yes! You can personalize the colors, text, and layout to match your event theme. Our templates are flexible and easy to edit.

Do my guests need to create an account?

No, guests don’t need to register. They simply click the link and get instant access to your event details.

Can I update event details after sending the invitation?

Absolutely. You can edit the time, location, or other details anytime, and your guests will see the updated information immediately.

Is there a limit to the number of guests I can invite?

No, you can invite as many guests as you like. The system is built to handle events of all sizes.

When does the processing time start?

The processing time begins the next business day after we receive your completed order form. From that moment, the estimated delivery time will be calculated.
